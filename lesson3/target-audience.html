Audience

The audience for this website is primarily farmers. Whether in the dairy industry or growing potatoes, these farmers depend on the weather forecast to make decisions in their daily activities. These farmers mostly live in small communities in this somewhat remote area of Idaho. They will also come to this website to find out about events and activities of the region in which they may have interest.

Personas

Howard Jensen is a 48-year-old Dairy farmer who runs a herd of 200 head of cattle. He comes to the site daily to see what days will bring inclement weather. He has a small family that wants to participate in community events that will allow them to make friends.

Joan Barton is a 68-year-old Potato Farmer. Her older children help her run the farm. She comes to the site to know the best time for plowing, weeding, spraying, and harvesting. She has grandchildren and she likes to attend events in which they participate.

Scenarios

  1. Howard has wants to know when a big snowstorm is coming, so he can put up feed and shelter his cattle.
  2. Howard wants to know when the county rodeo is being held.
  3. Joan wants to know when there is a freeze, so she can be sure to complete her fall harvest.
  4. Joan wants to know when the school is holding their annual holiday pageant, so she can attend and watch her grandchildren.
  5. Joan wants to know when it is going to rain next, so she can determine when the best time to spray her crops will be.
