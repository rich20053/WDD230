Audience

The audience for this website is individuals, families, and businesses who want to know more about commerce in Provo, Utah. The lifeblood of any community is commerce. Whether providing for the families that already live here or establishing new areas of growth for the community, businesses must exist to support the community. This site will aid businesses in working together to strengthen business interests in Provo. In doing this, Provo will become a more attractive place for people and their families.

Personas

Howard Jensen is a 48-year-old father of three who is thinking about starting his own business doing landscaping. He comes to the site to see how mamy homeowners there are that might need his services. He also comes to see what businesses might be his competitors. He plans to join the chamber so that he can be on the list of businesses that members of the community support.

Joan Barton is a 68-year-old grandmother. She comes to the site to see what services are available to her daughter, who is a single mother. She comes to look for certified baby sitting services and home cleaning services to lighten her daughter's burden. She and her daughter come to prospect for businesses that might be hiring.

Scenarios

  1. Howard wants to know what other businesses provide landscaping services, so he can determine if there is room in the market for his new business.
  2. Howard wants to know how many homeowners live in Provo, so he can determine if his business is feasible.
  3. Howard wants to join the chamber of commerce, so he can be listed where members of the community will see him to hire him.
  4. Joan wants to know what child care services are available in the community, so she can help her daughter get the child care help she needs.
  5. Joan wants to know what home care services are available in the community, so she can help her daughter get the home care help she needs.
  6. Joan wants to know what buinesses are in the community, so she can find a job or help her daughter find a job.
